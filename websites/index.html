Common Questions

What Clients Ask Us

How long does it take to build my website?
Most sites are live within 2–3 weeks from kickoff. We start with a design review, gather your content, and iterate quickly. If you have an urgent launch date, let us know — we can often accommodate it.
Do I own my website if I cancel?
Yes. On Professional and Enterprise plans, you own the design and can export your site if you choose to leave. We'll provide all source files and transfer your domain. Starter plan sites are hosted on our infrastructure.
What if I already have a website?
We can either rebuild it from scratch or add Dapper ID™ to your existing site. We'll audit your current site for free and recommend the best path forward.
What's included in "content updates"?
Text changes, image swaps, adding new service listings, updating hours or pricing, adding team members — anything that keeps your site current. We respond to update requests within 1 business day.
Do I need to provide my own copy and images?
On Starter, yes. On Professional and above, we include basic copywriting and source stock images or request yours during onboarding. We'll guide you either way — we've done this hundreds of times.
