Common Questions

What Dealers Ask Us

How does Dapper ID™ identify visitors without a form fill?
We use a combination of IP resolution, probabilistic matching, and identity graph technology to match website visitors to real consumer records. It's the same tech used by enterprise marketing platforms — now available for dealers of all sizes.
Is this compliant with privacy regulations?
Yes. Our data practices align with applicable federal and state regulations. All records in our system are sourced from opted-in or publicly available data, and we maintain strict data hygiene protocols including DNC scrubbing.
Do I need a big CRM or tech setup to use this?
No. We deliver data as a clean spreadsheet or directly to your existing CRM. Most dealers are up and running in under a week — no IT team required.
What kind of results can I expect?
Results vary by market size, current web traffic, and how quickly your team follows up. Our pilot clients in the golf cart vertical have seen 15–30 new identified contacts per month from existing traffic alone — before any cold outreach from Dapper IQ™ data.
Do I need to run ads to make this work?
No. Dapper ID™ works on any existing traffic — organic, referral, or paid. Dapper IQ™ is entirely independent of ad platforms. You already have buyers — we just help you find them.
